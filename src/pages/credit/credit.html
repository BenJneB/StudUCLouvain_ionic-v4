UCLcampus is an application designed by students to accompany UCL students in their daily life on campus.

The application is the subject of a memory directed by Marchesini Bruno and Daubry Benjamin supervised by their promoter Deville Yves.

Contributions are welcome for any good feature. This project being open-source, if you want to participate in its development you will find the public github via this link: https://github.com/bmarchesini/UCLCampus

For any additional information, it is possible to contact us on these two email addresses: bruno.marchesini@student.uclouvain.be benjamin.daubry@student.uclouvain.be

UCLCampus is available under the GNU/GPLv3 license.
